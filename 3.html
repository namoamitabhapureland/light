Meditation Guide
1. Every recitation of Amitabha transforms into Bodhi merit. Generating the supreme mind to explore the Lotus Store World also increases your spiritual progress.

2. The inner golden lotus grows from this merit. From the initial Bodhicitta, it transforms layer by layer, ultimately reaching Perfect Radiant Gold.

3. The Nine Luminaries manifest gradually as the practitioner accumulates the total count of sacred recitations and scriptures, interweaving their orbits until perfect completion.

4. If the practitioner abides daily in this quiet room, reflecting inwardly, the Dharma Realm will bestow formless Dharma tools according to conditions.

5. This quiet room is your private mustard-seed dojo. All data resides solely within your personal device, completely offline. Yet, as all things are impermanent, please remember to use "Backup Merit" in the main dojo to safeguard your spiritual journey.
